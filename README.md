# Customize Your Birthday Website

Welcome to your birthday website! This guide will help you customize it with your favorite photos and music.

## Adding Photos
1. **Upload Your Photos**  
   Go to the `assets/images` folder in your repository. You can add your favorite birthday photos here.
2. **Edit the Photo References**  
   Open the `index.html` file. Look for the `img` tags and replace the `src` attribute with the path to your uploaded images.

## Adding Music
1. **Upload Your Music Files**  
   Go to the `assets/music` folder in your repository. Upload the music files you'd like to play on the website.
2. **Edit the Music References**  
   Open the `index.html` file again. Look for the `audio` tags and replace the `src` attribute with the path to your uploaded music files.

## Preview Your Changes  
Once you've uploaded your files and edited the necessary references, open the `index.html` in your browser to preview the changes you made!  

Enjoy customizing your birthday website!
